Top three activities to do in Cologne

Attend a mess or climb to the top of the Cologne Cathedral

Our piece of Cultural Heritage will awe you wether through it's 700 years of history or through the view on the city from on top.

A favorite of tourists and locals alike: the Chocolate Museum

Learn about possibly the most favorite treat in the world and round it all up in by having a treat from the chocolate fountain at the end.

The museum of sports and our fair

Be engrossed in our museum of sports during the day and stunned by the light filled look of our city at night while enjoying our fair
